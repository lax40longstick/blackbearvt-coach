Privacy Policy

Effective: April 27, 2026

We collect account information, team/workspace information, practice plans, drill usage, billing metadata, analytics events, and support messages needed to operate Bear Den Coach HQ.

How we use information

We use information to provide the service, authenticate users, process billing, generate practice plans, improve product quality, prevent abuse, and respond to support requests.

Vendors

The app may use Supabase for authentication/data, Stripe for billing, OpenAI for AI plan generation, Sentry for monitoring, PostHog for analytics, Netlify for hosting/functions, and Cloudflare Turnstile for abuse prevention.

AI content

AI practice requests may be sent to an AI provider together with relevant drill-library data. Do not enter sensitive player medical information or private personal information into AI prompts.

Sharing

Public practice links can be viewed by anyone with the link. Keep private plans unshared unless you intend to distribute them.

Choices

You may request account or data assistance through the contact page. Some information must be retained for security, billing, or legal compliance.

Have counsel review before public launch.
