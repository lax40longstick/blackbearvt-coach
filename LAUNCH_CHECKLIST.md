# Bear Den Coach HQ Launch Checklist

## Product

- Public home page reviewed
- Pricing confirmed
- Trial signup connected to backend
- Account and onboarding pages connected to live data
- Existing coach workspace reachable at `/app.html`

## Engineering

- Replace local-only auth/config flow
- Lock down Firestore or move to managed backend
- Add monitoring, analytics, and uptime checks
- Add automated deploy pipeline
- Add smoke tests for signup, billing, and practice save flows

## Commercial

- Stripe products and prices created
- Tax and invoice settings reviewed
- Support email and help flow published
- Privacy policy reviewed by counsel
- Terms reviewed by counsel

## Pilot

- Recruit 3-5 coaches
- Run onboarding call script
- Capture setup friction and feature requests
- Track retention after week 1 and week 3
