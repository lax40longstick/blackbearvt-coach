# BenchBoss Coach HQ v0.10.0 Pilot Program

## Goal

Run a focused private beta with 3-5 real hockey coaches and prove that BenchBoss saves time before practice and works at the rink.

## Ideal pilot cohort

- 3 solo youth coaches
- 1 team with multiple coaches/manager
- 1 club/director or association lead

## Pilot length

3 weeks.

## Success criteria

BenchBoss is ready to sell if the pilot shows:

- 70%+ of coaches preload Bench Mode before practice
- 50%+ use Bench Mode at the rink
- 40%+ swap or adjust at least one drill live
- 60%+ publish a recap/practice/lineup to parents
- Average coach-reported time saved is 20+ minutes per practice
- At least 2 coaches say they would pay for Team or Pro

## Weekly pilot plan

### Week 0 — Setup
- Create coach accounts.
- Import one roster.
- Add one GameSheet link or CSV.
- Create one test lineup.
- Set team branding.
- Walk each coach through Bench Mode preload.

### Week 1 — Practice planning
- Each coach generates or builds one practice.
- Coach exports/shares or preloads it.
- Collect friction points.

### Week 2 — Rink workflow
- Each coach uses Bench Mode during one real practice.
- Track: bench_mode_opened, drill_swapped, practice_completed.
- Ask if large buttons, contrast, timer, and animations worked under rink conditions.

### Week 3 — Team hub
- Manager/coach publishes lineup or recap.
- Parent/assistant opens parent portal.
- Track: parent_recap_viewed.
- Ask what parents/managers wanted but could not find.

## Must-have feedback questions

1. Did BenchBoss save you time before practice? How many minutes?
2. Did Bench Mode work at the rink with your real phone/tablet?
3. Were the drills useful enough to run with your team?
4. Did the animations match the drill?
5. What confused you?
6. What would stop you from paying?
7. Which tier would you pay for: Free, Coach Pro, Team, Club?
8. What one feature would make this a must-have?

## Pilot deliverables

At the end of the pilot, create:

- 3 coach quotes
- 1 screenshot of Bench Mode in use
- 1 sample parent recap
- 1 case study: “How BenchBoss saved X minutes/week”
- top 10 bugs/friction points
- top 10 requested drills/features
