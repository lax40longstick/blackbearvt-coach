# Bear Den Coach HQ Pilot Program

## Goal

Validate whether youth hockey coaches will pay for practice planning and team operations in one tool.

## Pilot cohort

- 3 solo head coaches
- 1 staff-led team with assistants
- 1 club director or hockey operations lead

## What to measure

- Time to first saved practice
- Number of saved plans in first 14 days
- Staff invites sent
- Return usage on practice day
- Willingness to pay at trial end

## Interview questions

1. What did you replace with Bear Den Coach HQ?
2. Where did setup feel slow or confusing?
3. Which screen gave you the most immediate value?
4. What would block you from paying monthly?
5. Which features would make this useful across a full season?

## Success bar

- At least 3 pilot users save 2+ practices
- At least 2 say they would pay at the Team tier or higher
- No unresolved trust issue around data loss or account ownership
